CONTEXT

Diabetes is a condition in wich the body does not produce nough insuline (or nothing at all), needeed to convert the body's sugar to energy. Wich causes the glucose in the blood to be high.

There are 3 types :

Type 1 : do not produce insulin


Type 2 : do not responde as well to insulin


Gestational : during pregnancy, reversible


Diabetes is a degenerative desease. A person with diabetes can have a health team of up to 5 members. By 2040, his will be the reality of 71.1 million adults in Europe.

The goal here is to create an app that allows people with diabetes to keep track of their treatment and understand how they respond to it. At the same time, letting them share informations with their medical team and closed ones.

For whom ?
(obviously) People with diabetes, habits are hard to change so this app will be for a young population among the diabetes people, the medical follow up can be a factor of stress also.
Health team members, the follow up is important and the team can be made of 5 members so there is a need for organisation and communication.
Closed ones, diabetes is a familly disease in most of the case and being informed can be important.
