CONTEXT

49% of second hand consumers are age between 18 - 37 y.
43% of french web female mode consumers buy outfits and accessories from second hand
Values, convictions, why ?
*Ethic
*Accessibility : 50% of women whom are second hand consumers do it 'cause it's an opportunity to buy quality products cheaper.
*Image : The second hand buy today spread everywhere. It's a new way of consuming and finding unique products.
*Exclusivity : More rare products or big brand products, old editions

In 2025, Millennials will represent 45 % of second hand luxury consumers. 50% of salers are buyers.
Convictions, Pourquoi ?
*Finance : The sale of mode products offer a new and supplementary salary. *Re-Investing in new : whom sales to buy new products in stores. Some salers during the interviews said, 32%, that they sold mostly to buy new stuff. 70% of the sales are created by salers whom do not buy a lot of second hand products.
*Ethic : The environment issue is an important factor here for consumers.
*Habits
