SPIRIT

Joyfulness, friendlyness and respect are the first values that jumps on you when you arrive into the PNY universe. So much love and ideas between people, burgers and fries !

It's more a big diversified family than a corporation. And one of the goal is that the client wants to be part of this particular family. Of course everybody (even the dogs) is welcome and that's the strong suit of this StartUp !

I had the opportunity to participate in the construction of this burger centric world, as a designer and a communication manager. The PNY instagram page is colorful and full of jokes, songs or love messages to the members of the staff.

More than Instagram and the fast of beautifulness, a compagny such as ParisNewYork needed to be more present on Google and others apps relatives to food. As much as the SEO of the website that needed to be updated.
In this particular year that was 2020, you couldn't do anything without thinking about packaging design especially in the food industry. So I happened to design some of the burgers box for example. As a creative soul I also made graphics productions for outdoor display or indoor display.
