WHY MANI ?

The pollution is the first preoccupation for the 15-30y.

The digital world is the first cause of pollution in the world.
Yet there is no existence of a browser with the capacity of limitation against the digital pollution.

Datacenters use 11% of world's electricity Digital can be seen as the third most polluting state on earth 41 Billions kg of CO2 each year are ejected in the atmosphere by web users

The Web Browser is the first door into the digital world.

The secret for users fidelity with a web browser ? Answer their needs In 2008, users want speed, that was the sale argument of Google Chrome. At the end : 75% of retention.
